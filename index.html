Welcome to University Courtyard

The perfect place for married couples to live at BYU-Idaho

University Courtyard apartments has many features and amenities to offer you and your spouse. Located across the street from the BYU-Idaho campus, it is a fast and easy stroll to class. University Courtyard is just two blocks away from the new Rexburg Temple.

The newly constructed apartments at University Courtyard are affordable one bedroom apartments and can be fully furnished to make your stay more comfortable.

With over 90 apartments in 6 different buildings, University Courtyard (UC) is a small community. The social atmosphere is unique because 95% of our tenants are BYU-I students and most are newlyweds. You'll feel safe, comfortable and have a lot in common with everyone.

Residents enjoy activities sponsored by management such as BBQs, baby showers, march madness prizes, potluck dinners, half-price laundry days, free car wash days and even Halloween costume contests. Ask any of our tenants and they'll tell you that University Courtyard apartments is different for all the right reasons.

There are an abundance of parking spaces for families with one or two cars. The UC managers live on-site to take care of any maintenance or apartment needs.
