Application Instructions

If you meet the requirements on the right, email your resume to randy@ucourtyard.com.

Manager Application Requirements

  • You are willing to sign an employment contract starting July 1st 2011 and ending July 1st 2013.
  • You are able to submit a $500 refundable performance bond.
  • You are willing to be interrupted anytime, day or night to help tenants.
  • Perform 30 hours of office work per week sometime between 9am and 6pm. Monday thru Friday.
  • Personal requirements are, computer skills, people & management skills, personal achievement skills or talents. Have lots of patience and a steady, even temperament.
  • Managers apartment is a large one bedroom unit. Can your family and belongings fit?
  • Compensation is $1500/month. $850 cash, $650 rent & utilities.
